# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

This project is a friendly, welcoming space. Everyone who
participates — players, contributors, maintainers — is expected to
help keep it that way, regardless of background or identity.

We adopt the **Contributor Covenant, version 2.1** as our code of
conduct. Please read the full text here:

> https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

In short: be respectful and constructive, assume good faith, and
keep interactions free of harassment. Maintainers may remove or
reject contributions, comments, and participants that don't follow
these standards.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, report it
privately to the maintainer:

- **Contact:** zarami12140@gmail.com

Reports are handled confidentially. The maintainer will review and
respond as promptly as circumstances allow.

## Scope

This applies in all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull
requests, and any official channels — and when an individual is
representing the project in public spaces.
